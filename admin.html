EmailJS Configuration
⚠️ EmailJS must be configured for approval and rejection emails to be sent automatically. Follow the steps below.
📋
EmailJS Setup Guide
  1. Go to emailjs.com and create a free account.
  2. Under Email Services, add your Gmail account (jrraman@gmail.com) and note the Service ID.
  3. Under Email Templates, create three templates:
Template 1 — Request Notification (to you)

Subject: New Vendor Access Request – {{vendor_company}}
Body: Name: {{vendor_name}}, Email: {{vendor_email}}, Company: {{vendor_company}}, Reason: {{vendor_reason}}
To: jrraman@gmail.com

Template 2 — Approval (to vendor)

Subject: DEWA Portal – Access Approved ✅
Body: Dear {{vendor_name}}, your access has been approved.
Login URL: {{portal_url}} · Email: {{vendor_email}} · Access Code: {{access_code}}
To: {{to_email}}

Template 3 — Rejection (to vendor)

Subject: DEWA Portal – Access Request Update
Body: Dear {{vendor_name}}, your request has been declined. Reason: {{rejection_reason}}
To: {{to_email}}

  1. Copy your Public Key from the Account section.
  2. Enter all IDs in the Settings form above and click Save Settings.
